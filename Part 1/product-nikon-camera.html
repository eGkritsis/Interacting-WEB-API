Description

The Z fc is where classic Nikon camera design meets the innovative technology of the Z series, ensuring unparalleled image quality combined with pure, authentic design. I excels in any shooting environment, whatever your need may be: from getting that perfect night shot, to capturing the perfect video for a vlog. Lead your creativity to the new era with this lightweight DX-format mirrorless camera, bringing iconic style to your hands.


Design - it's in its DNA.

Black body with embossed texture. A metal frame with discreet feel. The Z fc derives its style from the classic icons that where the Nikon SLR film cameras, with its special edition NIKKOR Z lens embodying that design philosophy. Every aspect of the camera encompasses design ques to the pass. And the creative power of the present.

Physical options control

Distinct physical selectors to control shutter speed, exposure and ISO sensitivity, all without the need to access the camera's menu.

Upper information display

Need to capture portraits with a shallow depth of field or urban scenes from wide angles, while maintaining clarity throughout the frame? The small display enables you to confirm correct aperture selection at a glance by displaying the f/stop value on each capture.

Round electronic viewfinder

The Z fc has a high-resolution electronic viewfinder which is shaped round, drawing inspiration from the optical viewfinder of the Nikon FM2. It allows clear viewing during operation, and is automatically activated when you bring the camera near your eye.

Made for the present

The Z fc looks like it was designed in the '80s, however its sturdy frame made of magnesium alloy guarantees a camera that can stand up to today's demands. Robust in every way, the camera's main body is also lightweight and incredibly portable. Carry this camera around on your shoulder without worries, and be prepared to not fly under the radar.

Quality you can feel

The Z fc's frame is constructed from high-grade magnesium alloy, the same material used in all Nikon's professional range cameras. High in strength and low in weight, it is a camera you can worrylessly take with you wherever you inspiration leads you.
